1. Introduction

Welcome to EfficientTutor ("we," "our," or "us"). We recognize the importance of your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us.

This Privacy Policy articulates how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed when you access or use our tuition management application and website. By using our Service, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of data we collect include:

A. Personal Data

While using our Service, you may provide us with certain personally identifiable information, including but not limited to:

  • Identity Data: Full name, email address, and user role (Teacher, Parent, or Student).
  • Academic Data: Tuition schedules, subject details, session logs, and academic notes entered by you or your connected tutor/student.
  • Financial Records: Logs of fees due, payments made, and outstanding balances. Note: We do not store credit card numbers or process payments directly. We only store the records you manually enter for tracking purposes.

B. Usage & Device Data

We automatically collect certain information when you access the Service, such as:

  • Device type (e.g., mobile, desktop), operating system, and browser version.
  • Time zone settings and language preferences.
  • Interaction data, such as pages visited and features used, to help us improve user experience.

3. How We Use Your Information

We utilize the collected data for specific, legitimate business purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To create schedules, calculate fees, and sync data between linked Teacher and Student accounts.
  • Account Management: To manage your registration, authenticate your login, and maintain account security.
  • Communication: To contact you regarding critical updates, security alerts, or support inquiries.

4. Sharing of Information

We strictly do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share information only in the following scenarios:

  • Collaborative Features: Data entered by a Teacher (e.g., a scheduled class) is visible to the specific Student or Parent linked to that class.
  • Service Providers: We engage trusted third-party companies (such as Render.com for hosting and infrastructure) to facilitate our Service. These parties have access to your data only to perform specific tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your data if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

5. Data Storage and Security

Your data is securely stored on cloud infrastructure hosted by Render.com. We employ industry-standard security measures, including encryption in transit (SSL/TLS), to protect your information.

Please note that while we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your Personal Data, no method of transmission over the Internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service allows for "Student" accounts which may be used by individuals under the age of 18. We require that any account for a child under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your country) be created and managed by a Parent or Legal Guardian.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with Personal Data without your consent, please contact us immediately so that we can take necessary actions.

7. Your Data Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your data:

  • Access: The right to request copies of your personal data.
  • Rectification: The right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate.
  • Deletion: The right to request that we erase your personal data ("Right to be Forgotten"), subject to certain conditions.
  • Portability: The right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or disputes regarding this Privacy Policy, please feel free to contact our support team.
